Meet The Chef

Michael Weirich

Executive Chef

Executive Chef Michael Weirich was born and raised in West Chester and currently resides in Indian Hill. The nineteen year-old is currently studying chemical engineering at Vanderbilt University in Nashville, Tennessee. With over ten years of cooking experience, his passion for all things culinary has been influenced by his unique experiences such as living in Singapore, learning from professional chefs, and thousands of hours of practice in the kitchen cooking for family, friends, and guests from several other countries. Michael has won several cooking competitions, shares his work on his dedicated food instagram (@mikeydubsfood), and is currently authoring his first cookbook, “Think Like a Chef to Cook Like One.”

This chef is ready for his next adventure. Join him for a once-in-alifetime dining experience right here in historic Indian Hill. This limited-time pop-up will accommodate just twelve customers every week who will enjoy a seasonal 8-course tasting menu in their choice of comfortable indoor or outdoor seating. A sample menu is included on the reverse of this flyer; it is subject to change based on the availability of top-quality ingredients. Chef Weirich is meticulous in ensuring only the finest components make up each dish for peak quality and flavor.
